What is a Cleric?

The Cleric is a villager profession in Minecraft, recognized by their distinctive purple robes with a creeper emblem on the back. They work at a Brewing Stand and specialize in magical and potion-related items.

Clerics are the only villager that trades Ender Pearls, Bottles o' Enchanting, and Glowstone — making them essential for end-game progression, XP farming, and brewing.

Combined with a zombie farm producing Rotten Flesh, Clerics become an infinite Emerald source and your gateway to The End.

Pro Tips & Tricks

Trading Hall Setup

  • Build a zombie spawner farm for infinite Rotten Flesh
  • Line up multiple Clerics for bulk Emerald income
  • Each Cleric needs their own Brewing Stand and bed
  • Enclose them safely so they can't wander off

Rerolling Trades

If a Novice Cleric's trades aren't what you want, break their Brewing Stand and place it again. They'll reset to a new random offer.

Warning: Once you trade even once, their offers lock permanently.

Cleric vs. Other Villagers

Clerics sell consumable items you'll buy repeatedly — Ender Pearls, XP bottles, Glowstone. Librarians sell books you only need once. For long-term value, Clerics win.

Max Discount Strategy

  • Cure a Zombie Villager to get discounts
  • Zombify and re-cure them up to 5 times
  • Ender Pearls drop to 1 Emerald
  • XP Bottles drop to 1 Emerald
Nether Wart Farm: At Master level, Clerics buy 22 Nether Wart for 1 Emerald. Plant Nether Wart on Soul Sand for a passive Emerald farm alongside your Rotten Flesh income.
